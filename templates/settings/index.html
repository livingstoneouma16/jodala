Email Notifications (Resend)
Sends emails for loan approvals, disbursements, repayment receipts, overdue reminders, and new registrations
Get an API key at resend.com/api-keys, and make sure the From Email is on a domain you've verified with Resend (or use their onboarding@resend.dev address for quick testing -- it only delivers to your own inbox). Sends over HTTPS, so unlike SMTP-based email this works on Render and every other PaaS host.
SMS Notifications (Africa's Talking)
Texts members and clients directly for loan approvals, disbursements, repayments, savings deposits/withdrawals, overdue reminders, and new registrations
Create an account at africastalking.com and grab your Username and API Key from the dashboard. A free sandbox account works for testing but won't actually deliver to real phones — use a live app + a purchased/registered Sender ID to send real SMS.

M-Pesa STK Push (Lipa Na M-Pesa Online)
Lets staff trigger a payment prompt on a member's phone for loan repayments and savings deposits
Running against Safaricom's sandbox using their public test shortcode/passkey. Set your own Consumer Key & Secret below (from developer.safaricom.co.ke) to send real sandbox test pushes, then switch to Production with your live shortcode when ready to go live.
Safaricom must be able to reach your Callback URL over the public internet with a valid HTTPS certificate. If this app is only running locally, use a tunnel (e.g. ngrok) and paste its HTTPS URL + /mpesa/callback below — otherwise payments will show "pending" forever since the confirmation can never arrive.
M-Pesa B2C (Loan Disbursement)
Lets staff send loan funds directly to a member's phone instead of cash/bank
B2C has no shared sandbox default — even for testing you need your own Initiator Name, Initiator Password, and Certificate from your Daraja app's test credentials page, plus a shortcode approved for B2C ("Go Live for B2C"). The Consumer Key/Secret above are reused for B2C too.
